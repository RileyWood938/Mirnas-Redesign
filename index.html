Mirna's Cuban Cuisine

Mirna's Cuban Cuisine offers a unique dining experience that you won’t find elsewhere in Sarasota! We bring you delicious Cuban cuisine that is guaranteed to make your taste buds sing.


We pride ourselves in our ability to craft all of our food from scratch, and using only fresh and natural ingredients. We offer the best price, are a family restaurant and everything is made to order—even the desserts are made fresh. Our customer satisfaction is our number No.1 concern. We want the customer to feel like if they had travel to Cuba to eat our authentic cuban cuisine.


While our food is fantastic, it’s our service that really sets us apart. You come for the Cuban cuisine, but you stay for our quality, generous portions, moderate pricing and friendly attitude. We fully embrace the Cuban lifestyle and our restaurant is designed to reflect the ethnic and cultural themes in a way that our guests will love.


For unique and tasty dining, come try Mirna's Cuban Cuisine’s Cuban food in Sarasota, FL today, or call our Cuban restaurant about our catering options!
